Editorial Coverage

What We Cover & Offer

From editorial coverage of the latest research to hands-on fabrication services, 3D Print Ceramic supports the full lifecycle of ceramic additive manufacturing.

🏺

Direct Ink Writing (DIW)

Clay-body extrusion for functional and decorative ceramics. Supports stoneware, porcelain, and technical paste systems.

💡

LCM / DLP Ceramic

Lithography-based ceramic manufacturing for high-resolution alumina and zirconia parts with ±0.1 mm tolerances.

🔥

Firing & Post-Processing

In-house debinding and sintering. Glazing, surface finishing, and quality inspection to final spec.

📊

Prototyping & R&D

Rapid iteration for materials scientists and designers. Custom paste formulation available on request.

Our Process

How We Work

1

Design Review

We evaluate your CAD geometry for printability — wall thickness, unsupported spans, and shrinkage compensation for your target ceramic system.

2

Material Selection

Choose from stoneware and porcelain pastes for DIW, or alumina/zirconia slurries for LCM/DLP. We advise on material-application fit.

3

Print & Green Body

Parts are printed on our in-house equipment and inspected before moving to thermal processing.

4

Firing & Sintering

Controlled debinding and sintering cycles tailored to your material system. HIP post-processing available for critical applications.

5

Inspection & Delivery

Dimensional inspection, surface finish review, and photography. Parts shipped with full documentation.
