Ceramic Printing Services

01

DLP Ceramic Printing

Vat photopolymerization with ceramic-loaded resins delivers optical surface quality. Alumina and zirconia at 0.1mm resolution — ideal for dental prosthetics, fuel cell components, and precision insulators.

02

Binder Jetting

High-throughput ceramic production via powder-bed binder jetting. Silica sand, alumina, and silicon carbide sintered to full density. Complex cores and investment casting shells without tooling.

03

Sintering & Finishing

Complete post-processing: debinding, sintering to 1600°C, HIP densification, and precision grinding. Dimensional inspection and porosity testing ensure production-grade consistency.

MATERIALS

Ceramic Material Capabilities

Technical ceramics for demanding environments — where metals corrode and polymers fail, ceramics deliver stability and chemical resistance.

  • ● Alumina (Al₂O₃) — electrical insulation and wear resistance
  • ● Zirconia (ZrO₂) — dental crowns, oxygen sensors, thermal barrier
  • ● Silicon carbide — extreme heat and abrasion resistance
  • ● Silica — precision casting cores and investment mold inserts
  • ● Hydroxyapatite — biocompatible bone scaffold material
  • ● Custom ceramic blends available on request
